Gives a description of the position of 'intermediate wholesalers' in the general market structure. This is a relatively detailed account of just this group of people, not an overview of the whole market structure.

The text is quite academic in nature, so you should really be interested in a socialogical/anthropological point of view if you plan on reading the book.

The play "Sukeroku" mentioned in one of the footnotes can be found in "The Kabuki Handbook" by Aubrey & Giovanna Halford (an excellent book!).
