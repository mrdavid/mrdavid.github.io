    I am David and I currently live in Tokyo.

    Like most people, I love learning new things. But I have noticed that learning new things can be difficult:

    ·When you are working full time there will be days or weeks when you have to put your current project aside. If you do not keep track of things, you will find it hard to pick your project back up. You will lose time because you have to revisit material you have already studied.
    ·While the internet offers an immense amount of helpful information on any topic, the resources are scattered. An even bigger problem is that there is no guidance on where to start and how to progress. Only resources for absolute beginners give some indication of the required knowledge or some idea of what might come next.

    To help me and others with this problem, I use this space to document and share my own learning curve.
